    In Spain, fishing licenses are mainly divided into two categories: sea fishing licenses and river (freshwater) fishing licenses. This article will provide a detailed guide on how to apply for both types of licenses online.

    I. Sea Fishing License Application Process#

    üìù Application Steps#

    1. Log in to the application website using your personal electronic certificate.
    2. Fill out the form as instructed on the page (do not use Google Translate plugins, as this may cause submission errors).
    3. Some license types require additional documents. For example, underwater fishing requires a medical certificate, and boat fishing requires boat documentation.
    4. Pay the fee online (Visa is supported).
    5. You will receive the digital license within a few days after submission. A link to download the license will be sent to your email address.
