Explanation of Financial Requirements#

  • The non-lucrative visa renewal is for a 2-year period.
  • One year requires 4 times the IPREM, so two years require 8 times.
  • For each additional family member, an extra 1x IPREM is needed for one year, and 2x for two years.

Taking the official 2025 data as an example: IPREM = ‚Ç¨7,200 / year

II. Important Notes (Must Read)#

  • It is recommended to use a collective residence certificate (Empadronamiento colectivo). How to apply for a “Collective Residence Certificate” online
  • The insurance certificate must cover the entire residency period. Health insurance cannot be interrupted during the residency.
  • All documents are recommended to be issued within the last 3 months.
  • Documents of the same type can be merged into a single PDF.
  • The size of a single file is recommended to be no more than 6MB.
  • It is recommended that adults submit their applications separately using their own digital certificates; otherwise, a letter of authorization must be submitted.

V. Core Rules (Please Remember)#

  • One person, one case: A family of three = 3 EX01 applications
  • Applications for minors must be handled by a legal guardian.
  • An adult spouse can handle the submission, but it is recommended to provide a letter of authorization.
