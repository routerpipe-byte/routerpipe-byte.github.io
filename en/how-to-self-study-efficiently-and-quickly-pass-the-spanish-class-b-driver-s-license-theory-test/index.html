How to Self-Study Efficiently and Quickly Pass the Spanish Class B Driver's License Theory Test

February 4, 2026 · 6 min | Translations:
  • Zh
  • Es

I. Theory Knowledge Study Method#

Directly cramming with practice questions is highly discouraged because:

  1. The question bank is vast. The English version has over 200 sets, each with 30 questions, totaling more than 6,000 questions. Completing them all is time-consuming and exhausting.
  2. The translations and questions in the bank may not be accurate, and the answers could be incorrect.
  3. The actual exam questions are not taken directly from the question bank, so finishing it is not a guarantee of success.
  4. The knowledge in the question bank is fragmented and unsystematic. Without systematic learning, you might be unable to answer if a question is phrased differently.

Efficient Study Method: Since I took the exam in English, the following information is based on English materials.

  • Start with condensed content from the textbook: https://practicatest.com/en/book-theory-driving-test. The information on this webpage is very concise and can be reviewed in about 4 hours. However, it lacks content on new energy vehicles, environmental labels, electronic motorcycle helmets, and electric scooters, which you’ll need to study from the textbook.
  • Complete Theory Textbook: Most textbooks are over 200 pages long, containing a lot of content, which can be time-consuming and tiring to study. It’s recommended to supplement your learning by focusing on your weak areas while doing practice questions. If you have time, you can skim through the entire textbook. Digital versions of the English textbook are circulated privately, but due to copyright issues, I can only provide keywords for you to search on your own. You can also buy a physical copy on Amazon. Keywords: “Manual Permiso B inglés”. Online digital English textbook: https://fliphtml5.com/lptj/szub/basic/51-100

Popular Question Banks#

  • Popular options include Practicatest, AEOL, and Todotest, all available for online purchase. Taking AEOL as an example:
    • Purchase Link: https://www.autoescueladummy.com/product-page/plan-premium
    • Online Question Bank Link: https://cloud.aeolservice.es/
    • It costs 10 euros per month. You need to enter your NIE number to link your account upon purchase. To renew after expiration, you can make another purchase and enter the same NIE number to extend your account.

AEOL Question Bank Usage Recommendations:#

  • Choose the 744 question bank and start with the last few sets of questions. The bank is not randomly arranged; the earlier sets contain older content, while the newer topics are in the later sets.
  • The latest topics (such as vehicle environmental labels, electronic motorcycle helmets, new energy vehicles, electric scooters, etc.) may only appear in the last few new test sets.
  • Older textbooks may also not cover these topics, so supplementary study is required.

Tips for Practice Questions:#

  • It’s not necessary to complete all the questions in the bank, as there are too many, and the actual exam questions are not identical to those in the bank.
  • The actual exam consists of 30 questions to be completed in 30 minutes, with a maximum of 3 incorrect answers allowed.
  • When your accuracy on practice questions reaches over 90%, your chances of passing the exam are very high.
  • The AEOL question bank records your incorrect answers. Use the incorrect answer test feature to review and reinforce your knowledge before the exam.

Free Mock Tests from the DGT:#

The DGT’s official website provides 3 free English mock tests for practice: https://sedeapl.dgt.gob.es:7443/WEB_AUTO-6/examen/Instrucciones.jsf

II. DIY Online Registration#

Booking an Appointment for the Theory Test#

  • Book an appointment on the official website: https://sede.dgt.gob.es/es/otros-tramites/cita-previa-jefaturas/index.shtml
  • Click on “Solicitud de cita previa” and follow the prompts to book.
  • Note: This is to book an appointment to go to the DGT office to apply for the exam, not to book the exam date itself.
  • Don’t worry if there are no available slots. They are usually released on weekdays at 2 PM. You will definitely get an appointment if you check for a few days.

Medical Examination for Driving Test:#

  • List of medical centers: https://www.dgt.es/conoce-la-dgt/con-quien-trabajamos/centros-reconocimiento-conductores/ Just choose the one closest to you.
  • Medical Examination Content:
    1. Inquiry about medical history;
    2. Concentration test (includes mini-games): A ball passes through an opaque box. You must judge the time it takes for the ball to pass through based on its speed and the distance to the sides of the box, then press a pause button. Separately control two cars with your left and right hands, driving them on two different tracks while avoiding hitting the edges;
    3. Vision test.
  • Note:
    • After the examination, double-check the information on the report (NIE number, name, date of birth, address, phone number, date of examination, etc.).
    • The medical report is valid for 3 months, so be sure to check the date of the examination.
    • The fee is around 30 euros, and you need to bring your NIE card.

Exam Fee Payment:#

  • Payment Website: https://sede.dgt.gob.es/es/otros-tramites/compra-y-actualizacion-de-tasas/
  • You can also directly access the payment form: https://sedeclave.dgt.gob.es/WEB_Tasas7/jsp/tasas/generarPresupuesto.jspx
  • On the form, select “791-2.1”. Fill in the information as prompted and submit.
  • Payment methods: You can choose to pay online or in cash at a bank. Remember to print or save the receipt after payment. Online payments are supported by major local Spanish banks like BBVA, Caixa, etc. https://sede.agenciatributaria.gob.es/Sede/deudas-apremios-embargos-subastas/pagar-aplazar-consultar/listado-entidades-bancarias-adheridas.html?faqId=3a96573ba13d2710VgnVCM100000dc381e0aRCRD
  • The fee for 2024 is 94.05 euros.
  • This fee covers two exam attempts. If you fail the first time, you can go back to the office to book a second exam date. If you fail both attempts, you will need to pay the fee again.

On-site Document Preparation for Booking the Exam Date:#

Go to the DGT office at your scheduled appointment time. Enter your NIE number at the ticket machine to get a number. Submit the following documents to book your exam date on-site.

  1. Appointment confirmation (printed or saved on your phone).
  2. NIE card (must be valid).
  3. Application form “Solicitud de pruebas de aptitude” (make sure to fill in the correct exam language; otherwise, you might receive the Spanish test instead of the English one). https://sede.dgt.gob.es/export/sites/dgt/.galleries/modelos-solicitud/04/Mod.04-ES.pdf
  4. Medical report (within 3 months).
  5. Proof of residence (empadronamiento) (within 3 months, can be printed from your local city hall’s website using a digital certificate, e.g., for Rocafort, it’s the Rocafort city hall, not the Valencia city hall).
  6. Passport-sized photo with a white background (32x26mm, 1 piece, not always required).
  7. Payment form “791-2.1” and the payment receipt.

III. Exam Day Instructions#

  • It is recommended to arrive at the test center half an hour early, bringing your NIE card and all registration documents.
  • An examiner will call out names at the entrance to the exam room. You will enter in order.
  • The exam is computer-based:
    • Enter your NIE number to log into the exam system.
    • The exam duration is 30 minutes, with a total of 30 questions.

IV. Checking Theory Test Results#

  • Check via Mobile App: Use the miDGT mobile app to check your results. Log in by linking your digital certificate; the certificate used on your computer can be imported to your phone. Results are typically available about 1 hour after the exam.
  • Check on the Official Website: Results are usually available after 10 hours. Results inquiry page: https://sedeclave.dgt.gob.es/WEB_NOTP_CONSULTA/consultaNota.faces

Finally, good luck, and I hope you pass the Spanish Class B driver’s license theory test soon. Note that you must pass the practical test within 3 years, or your theory test result will expire.
