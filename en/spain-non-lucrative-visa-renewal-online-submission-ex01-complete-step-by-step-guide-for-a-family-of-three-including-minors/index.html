    Financial Requirements Explanation

    • Non-lucrative visa renewal is for a 2-year period.
    • One year requires 4 times the IPREM, so two years require 8 times.
    • For each additional family member, an extra 1x IPREM is needed for 1 year, and 2x for 2 years.

    Taking the official 2025 figures as an example: IPREM = ‚Ç¨7,200 / year

    2. Important Notes (Must Read)

    • It is recommended to use a collective residence certificate How to apply for a “Collective Residence Certificate” online (Empadronamiento colectivo).
    • The insurance certificate must cover the entire residency period. Health insurance must not be interrupted during the residency period.
    • All documents are recommended to be issued within the last 3 months.
    • Similar documents can be merged into a single PDF.
    • The size of a single file is recommended to be no more than 6MB.
    • It is recommended that adults submit their applications separately using their own digital certificates; otherwise, a power of attorney must be submitted.

    5. Core Rules (Please Remember)

    • One person, one case: A family of three = 3 EX01 applications
    • Applications for minors must be submitted by a legal guardian.
    • An adult spouse can submit on behalf of the other, but it is recommended to include a power of attorney.
